##  About the Report

This group report examines whether Artificial Intelligence is a threat or a boon to humanity. 

The report discusses:

- Advantages of AI in healthcare, education, productivity, and security  
- Risks such as unemployment, privacy issues, ethical concerns, and misuse  
- Comparative analysis of both perspectives  
- Importance of regulation and ethical guidelines  
- Future prospects of Artificial Intelligence  

The study concludes that AI's impact depends on responsible innovation, ethical governance, and proper regulation.
